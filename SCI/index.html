What is Spinal Cord Injury (SCI)?

Spinal cord injury (SCI) usually produces sensory and/or mobility impairment, which often makes it more difficult for persons living with SCI to fully participate in society.

In mainstream U.S.A., full participation in community and society requires people to travel to some degree for work, school and l eisure. In many cases, however, travel may pose serious challenges for persons after SCI.
